USSD Assistant

USSD Assistant is a Utility app to help all subscribers to major network providers in Nigeria access easily, seamlessly and speedily all available USSD codes regardless of their network provider or number of sims presently installed on their Android device.
- It's easy and intuitive to use.
- Reduces the number of clicks required to dial a simple USSD Code.
- Flexible, as it offers you a one time save option of newly available USSD Codes, as well as delete or update existing codes.
- You never have to memorize a USSD Code as they are all at your finger tips and just a few clicks away.
- You stay updated with latest news, promos, etc. from you network provider through their homepage, without having to initiate your brower (Most network providers homepage are free to access without data).

  • Client: Personal Project
  • Date: October 2016
  • Service: Android Mobile Development

CGPA Manager

CGPA Manager is a personal application aimed at helping universities(college) students achieve suitable Grade Points by offering: A detailed break down of all academic work/results (Sessions to semesters to the individual courses involved).
- Time tables to ease scheduling of all courses and also reminder of courses, test and exams at the appropriate time.
- Progress chart to show a visual representation of a students academic progress.
- Transcripts (results sheets) generated from the app in common electronic forms.

  • Client: Personal Project
  • Date: October 2016
  • Service: Android Mobile Development
